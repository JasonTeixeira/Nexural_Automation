# Product context

## Mission

Nexural Automation is an evidence-first operating system for learning, building, validating,
and safely operating NinjaTrader 8 automation.

## Users

- NinjaScript learners who need executable, failure-driven instruction.
- Strategy engineers who require deterministic validation and realistic cost controls.
- Execution engineers building resilient Sim101/Playback101 bridges and order state machines.
- Maintainers who need reproducible packages, audit trails, and compatibility evidence.

## Product layers

1. **Learn** — executable labs, hidden tests, code-derived grading, and trace-aware feedback.
2. **Build** — strategies, indicators, AddOns, bridges, and reusable safety primitives.
3. **Validate** — Strategy Analyzer, walk-forward, Playback, cost, and overfitting evidence.
4. **Operate** — simulation-only reconciliation, telemetry, incident recovery, and kill switches.

## Non-goals

- Profitability claims or strategy recommendations.
- Browser-triggered or Academy-triggered live order routing.
- Treating historical, Playback, Sim101, and live results as equivalent.
- Marking external validation complete without independent evidence.

## 99+ outcome metrics

- 60 or more executable labs and at least four code-graded capstones.
- 100% machine-derived grading with no learner-declared success values.
- 95% or greater coverage of the published NT8 competency matrix.
- All shipping NinjaScript packages import and compile on every supported NT8 version.
- At least 20 deterministic execution incident profiles.
- 95% clean-machine setup success and median first passing lab under 10 minutes.
- Signed, checksummed, SBOM-attached, reproducible release artifacts.
- Independent beta evidence meeting the gate in [`beta/README.md`](../beta/README.md).
- Two independent Windows machines and two supported NT8 patch versions covering Playback and Simulator.
- Zero unresolved critical/high vulnerabilities plus an external security assessment.
- A second qualified non-owner maintainer for critical-path review.
