# World-class qualification track

## Objective

Turn the `99+` claim into a computed release state backed by independent NT8, learner,
security, maintainer, and supply-chain evidence. No standalone strategies are in scope.

## Invariants

1. A missing record is a failed gate, never an implied pass.
2. Distinct machines, testers, versions, learners, reviewers, and maintainers are counted by
   pseudonymous stable identifiers.
3. Playback101 and Sim101 evidence is required. Live-account evidence is rejected.
4. Stable release publication stops unless the complete qualification policy passes.
5. Automated evidence is tied to an immutable commit and generated by a named workflow run.
6. External evidence contains hashes and attestations, not brokerage credentials or raw trades.

## Acceptance criteria

- Two distinct Windows machines and two distinct independent testers.
- Two supported NinjaTrader patch versions with successful GUI import and global compile.
- Passing Playback101 and Sim101 scenario bundles.
- Disconnect detection within 5 seconds and restart recovery within 30 seconds.
- Reproducible, checksummed, SBOM-attached, signed and signature-verified artifacts.
- Zero unresolved critical or high vulnerabilities.
- At least 50,000 property cases, 50,000 fuzz cases and an 85% mutation score.
- 100 distinct learners, 25 code-graded capstone completers and three provider profiles.
- One independent external security review with no unresolved critical/high findings.
- Two qualified critical-path maintainers, including one maintainer other than the owner.

## Non-goals

- Live routing.
- Profitability certification.
- Manufacturing external evidence.
- Adding or expanding standalone strategies.
