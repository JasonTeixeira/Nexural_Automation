# Automation Glossary

## DSR

Deflated Sharpe Ratio. A Sharpe adjustment that accounts for non-normality and strategy selection bias.

## Walk-Forward

A validation process that fits on one window and evaluates on the next unseen window.

## PBO

Probability of Backtest Overfitting. A measure of how likely the selected strategy is curve-fit.

## Monte Carlo

Repeated simulation or resampling used to estimate distribution, drawdown, and tail behavior.

## CVaR

Conditional Value at Risk. The average loss inside the tail beyond a chosen VaR threshold.

## Slippage

Execution cost from getting filled worse than the assumed price.

## Bridge

A connector that moves paper signals, controls, or reconciliation data between systems.

## Kill Switch

A bridge control path that blocks or flattens activity when risk rules or operator controls require it.
