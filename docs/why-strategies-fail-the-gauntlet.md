# Why Strategies Fail The Gauntlet

## Not Enough Trades

The sample is too small to trust. Collect more trades or reduce the claim.

## Weak Deflated Sharpe

The apparent Sharpe may be a product of parameter search or non-normal returns.

## Poor Walk-Forward Efficiency

The strategy works in sample but fails when evaluated on unseen windows.

## Cost Stress Failure

The edge disappears after realistic commission and slippage assumptions.

## Tail Risk Failure

Drawdown, CVaR, or Monte Carlo outcomes are too severe for the stated objective.

## Concentration Failure

Profit comes from too few trades, days, regimes, or symbols.

## Correct Response

Do not lower thresholds to make the strategy pass. Write down the failure, change the thesis or implementation, and rerun the same gate.
